The Peniarth Arms is a family owned business. It has many things to offer, including a dining room area, bar, a beer garden and accommodation. Dogs are welcome in the Peniarth Arms, so enjoy a meal in the indoor bar area or outside at the outdoor bar hut.

The beer garden has a heated and covered area, which is perfect for those cooler damp evenings.
