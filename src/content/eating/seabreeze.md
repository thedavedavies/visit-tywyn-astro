Soak up a relaxed atmosphere and enjoy a seasonal selection of Welsh food and drink at this restaurant offering a feast of local produce. Their menus are changed seasonally to reflect what is available throughout the year. All of the produce is sourced from trusted and passionate individuals who care about the quality and standard of their produce.
