Whether you're waiting for a train, or just enjoying the sights - a stop at the King's Cafe at the <a href="/things-to-do/the-talyllyn-railway/">Talyllyn Railway</a> is a very enjoyable experience. The Kings Cafe at Tywyn Wharf Station is open all year round, and is very dog friendly - your dog is even welcome to travel with you on the train!
