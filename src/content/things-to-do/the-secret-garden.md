Come on a very special treasure hunt, and find The Secret Garden in Tywyn! Right next to the <a href="/things-to-do/magic-lantern-cinema/">Magic Lantern Cinema</a>, come and enjoy a cocktail, mocktails and cinema snacks.

More <a href="/dog-friendly-cafes/">dog friendly cafe's in Tywyn</a>.
<h2>Do I need to book the Secret Garden? ​</h2>
No, The Secret Garden doesn't take bookings. It’s first come first served unless you are bringing a party of 10 or more.
<h2>Can I bring children?​</h2>
Absolutely – children are very welcome.

<img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1279" src="/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/MagicLantern2-1024x681.jpg" alt="" width="640" height="426" />
<h2>Can I bring dogs to the Secret Garden?</h2>
Of course, but please keep them on a lead.​
<h2>How does it work?</h2>
When you arrive, simply find a table in the secret garden. Take a look at the menu on the table, make a note of your table name and then head to the cinema box office to place your order. The secret garden accept cash or cards. Your drinks and snacks will then be delivered to you at your table!

Why is the Secret Garden closed at certain times?

Because the Secret Garden is run by Tywyn Cinema, it's primarily a cinema and not a bar.

The opening times do state the periods each day when the bar is closed, and it's suggested you avoid arriving during this period - but please do feel free to come before and double up your order... You are very welcome to stay in the garden when the bar is closed.
