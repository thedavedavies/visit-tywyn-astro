<strong>Why visit Tywyn? Discover the incredible scenery and the <a href="/webcam">breathtaking sunsets</a>, the long beaches, the ancient history and the <a href="/eating/index.html">local delicacies</a>!</strong>
<h3>Frequently Asked Questions</h3>
<h4>What day is Tywyn Market?</h4>
Market Day is on a Monday, and runs from May to October.
<h4>What Supermarkets are there in Tywyn?</h4>
We have a Coop and a Spar in Tywyn – and Tywyn has a great local bakery!
<h4>Where is the Library?</h4>
<a title="Tywyn Library" href="https://www.gwynedd.gov.uk/en/Residents/Libraries-and-archives/Your-local-library/Tywyn-library.aspx" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Tywyn Library</a> is on Neptune Road, and has free public Wifi available.
<h4>Is there a Pharmacy?</h4>
Yes – there is a Rowlands Pharmacy on the High Street.
<h4>Where can I buy petrol</h4>
There is a petrol station right in the heart of Tywyn  at the Spar Garage on the High Street.
