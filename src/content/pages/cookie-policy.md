<h2>Cookie Policy for Visit Tywyn</h2>
<strong>Last updated: 01/02/2025</strong>

Like most websites, <strong>visit-tywyn.co.uk</strong> uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. This policy explains what cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.
<h3>1. What Are Cookies?</h3>
Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, analyse visitor behaviour, and improve functionality. Some cookies are essential for the site to work, while others help us improve user experience.
<h3>2. What Cookies Do We Use?</h3>
We use the following types of cookies:
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Strictly Necessary Cookies</strong>: Required for core website functions (e.g., security, accessibility, and form submissions). These cannot be disabled.</li>
 	<li><strong>Performance &amp; Analytics Cookies</strong>: Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics). These are optional.</li>
 	<li><strong>Functionality Cookies</strong>: Store user preferences, such as language settings.</li>
 	<li><strong>Marketing &amp; Tracking Cookies</strong>: Used by third parties to deliver targeted advertising (we currently do <strong>not</strong> use these).</li>
</ul>
<h3>3. Managing Cookies</h3>
You can manage or disable cookies in several ways:
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Via Our Cookie Banner</strong>: When you visit our site, you can choose which cookies to accept.</li>
 	<li><strong>Through Your Browser Settings</strong>: You can block or delete cookies in your browser. See the Help section of your browser for guidance.</li>
</ul>
For more information, visit <a href="https://www.aboutcookies.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">AboutCookies.org</a>.
<h3>4. Third-Party Cookies</h3>
We use third-party services that may place cookies on your device. These include:
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Google Analytics</strong>: Tracks visitor behaviour to improve our website. Data is anonymised.</li>
</ul>
For more details, please see Google's <a href="https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Cookie Policy</a>.
<h3>5. Changes to This Policy</h3>
We may update this policy to reflect changes in law or technology. Please check this page regularly for updates.
