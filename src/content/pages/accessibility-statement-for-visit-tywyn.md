<time datetime="2025-01-01">Last updated: 01/01/2025</time>

Accessibility is a core part of our commitment to an inclusive web. This statement outlines the steps we take to ensure a user-friendly experience for everyone, including those who rely on assistive technologies.

At <strong>Visit Tywyn</strong>, we are committed to ensuring our website is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of ability or technology. We strive to adhere to internationally recognised accessibility standards, including the <a href="https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1</a> at Level AA.

<section>
    <h2>Measures to Support Accessibility</h2>
    <p>Our website at <a href="/">Visit Tywyn</a> implements:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Semantic HTML to improve screen reader navigation</li>
      <li>Keyboard navigability, ensuring all interactive elements are accessible without a mouse</li>
      <li>Sufficient colour contrast, making content readable for people with visual impairments</li>
      <li>Alternative text for images to provide context for users of assistive technologies</li>
      <li>Adjustable text sizes through browser settings for better readability</li>
    </ul>
  </section>


<section>
    <h2>Ongoing Improvements</h2>
    <p>We recognise that web accessibility is an ongoing effort. To maintain and enhance usability, we partner with <a href="https://davedavies.dev">digital accessibility consultants</a> to conduct regular audits and implement best practices.</p>
  </section>

 <section>
    <h2>Known Issues</h2>
    <p>While we aim for full compliance, some areas may still present challenges:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Third-party content and embedded services may not be fully accessible</li>
      <li>Some older documents may not meet current accessibility standards</li>
    </ul>
    <p>If you experience difficulty accessing any content, please contact us using the details below, and we will work to resolve the issue.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h2>Contact Information</h2>
    <p>If you need information in an alternative format or have accessibility feedback:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Use our <a href="/contact/">online contact form</a></li>
    </ul>
    <p>We will make every reasonable effort to accommodate your needs.</p>
  </section>
