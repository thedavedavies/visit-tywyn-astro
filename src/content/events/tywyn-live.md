Tywyn Live brings a full day of free live music to the seafront each August bank holiday, with a brand new stage on the Promenade hosting a line-up that runs from lunchtime right through to the evening. Run by the volunteers of Tywyn Events Organisation, it has become one of the highlights of the town's summer: a relaxed, family-friendly day out with the sea as the backdrop.

Expect a real mix of styles across the day, from trad jazz and rock to indie, plus a set from the acclaimed Welsh beatboxer and live-looping artist Mr Phormula. Bring a chair or a picnic blanket, settle in on the prom, and follow Tywyn Events Organisation on Facebook for set times and any last-minute updates.
